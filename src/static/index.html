Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI Voice Assistant

What are the system requirements?

Our AI Voice Assistant works on Windows 10/11, macOS 10.15+, and Linux Ubuntu 18.04+. You'll need at least 8GB RAM, a microphone, and an internet connection for AI processing.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes! All screen capture and voice processing happens locally on your machine. Only anonymized queries are sent to our AI service, and no personal data or screen content is stored on our servers.

The Professional and Developer licenses include commercial use rights. The Basic license is for personal use only.

How do I get support?
