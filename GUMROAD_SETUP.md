## Gumroad Account Setup

### 1. Create Your Gumroad Account
1. Go to [gumroad.com](https://gumroad.com)
2. Click "Start Selling" and create your account
3. Complete your profile with professional information
4. Set up payment methods (PayPal, Stripe, or direct deposit)
5. Configure tax settings based on your location

**Creator Name**: Choose a professional name that builds trust
**Bio**: Write a compelling description of your expertise
